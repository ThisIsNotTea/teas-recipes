Zucchini Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchini, sliced thin and grilled to half their size
  • 1/2 to 1 pound of ground beef
  • 24 ounces spaghetti sauce
  • 1/4 onion, chopped (optional)
  • 4-6 mushrooms, chopped (optional)
  • 15 ounces ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Pepper to taste
  • A handful of fresh basil and parsley, chopped, or 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and mushrooms (if using) and cook until tender.
  3. Add the spaghetti sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta or cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and pepper.
  5. Gather and chop the basil and parsley (if using fresh).
  6. In a 9 by 13-inch glass dish, layer the ingredients as follows:
    • 1/3 of the zucchini slices
    • 1/2 of the ground beef mixture
    • 1/2 of the cottage cheese mixture
    • 1/3 of the shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1/3 of the chopped basil and parsley
    • Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of zucchini, parsley, and mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Notes

This recipe can easily be made vegetarian by omitting the ground beef.

If you don't have fresh basil and parsley, you can use 1 tablespoon of dried basil instead.
