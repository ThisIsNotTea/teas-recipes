Apple Cider Vinegar Expectorant Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp powdered ginger
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar

Instructions

  1. Heat the lemon juice and apple cider vinegar in a small saucepan over medium heat until it comes to a simmer.
  2. Add in the Cayenne pepper, powdered ginger, and honey. Stir until the honey is dissolved.
  3. Pour the mixture into a jar and let it cool to room temperature.

Notes

This expectorant can be stored in the refrigerator for up to one week.
