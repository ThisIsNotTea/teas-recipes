Bone Broth Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken carcasses, meat removed
  • Frozen vegetable scraps (optional)
  • 12-15 whole peppercorns
  • Water, enough to cover the chicken carcasses
  • 1 unpeeled crushed garlic clove (optional)
  • 1 cap full of apple cider vinegar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken carcasses and vegetable scraps in a large stock pot.
  2. Add peppercorns and enough water to cover the chicken.
  3. Bring up to 180-210F, but do not bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer covered for 9+ hours.
  5. If desired, add the unpeeled crushed garlic clove and apple cider vinegar during the last hour of cooking.

Serve hot and enjoy!
