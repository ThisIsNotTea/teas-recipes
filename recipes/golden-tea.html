Golden Tea

Ingredients:

  • Cloves, ground
  • Nutmeg, ground
  • Turmeric, ground
  • Cardamom whole, crushed (5)
  • Orange zest
  • Ginger, ground
  • Cinnamon, ground
  • Peppercorns, whole (4)
  • Honey, 2 spoons

Instructions:

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add the ground cloves, nutmeg, turmeric, cardamom, orange zest, ginger, cinnamon, and peppercorns.
  3. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and strain.
  5. Stir in 2 spoons of honey.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Notes:

This recipe makes 2 servings.

Feel free to adjust the amount of honey to your liking.
