L. Reuteri Yogurt

Serves: N/A

Total time: 36 hours

With Tablets

  • 10 tablets of BioGaia L. Reuteri strain
  • 2 tbsp of inulin powder
  • 32oz (1 quart) whole milk or half and half - cannot use low-fat option for either one

Instructions

  1. Crush tablets in a clean plastic bag.
  2. Add crushed tablets to a clean bowl along with the inulin powder.
  3. Add a small amount of milk/half and half and whisk to make a slurry.
  4. Add the remaining milk/half and half and whisk to combine.
  5. Put the mixture into a culturing device (instant pot/yogurt maker).
  6. Set the device at 97-100 degrees Fahrenheit. Do not exceed 105. 110 will kill the culture.
  7. Culture for 36 hours.
  8. The yogurt will be viable for ten days.

With Already-made Yogurt

  • 2 tbsp of cultured yogurt
  • 2 tbsp of inulin
  • 32oz (1 quart) full-fat dairy product

Instructions

  1. Combine 2 tbsp of cultured yogurt with 2 tbsp of inulin and a small amount of dairy.
  2. Combine into a slurry.
  3. Add the remaining dairy and whisk to combine.
  4. Place in a yogurt maker for 36 hours.
