Nettle Tea

Serves: N/A

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time: 10 minutes

Total time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Nettle plants (not roots)
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Pick nettle plants, wearing gloves to protect against stings. Select plants before they flower.
  2. Lay the nettle plants out on a screen to dry. Once dried, they will no longer sting.
  3. In a small canning pot, loosely fill it halfway with the dried nettle plants.
  4. Fill the pot with water, leaving about 2-3 inches of space from the top.
  5. Heat the pot until it is just about to boil, then turn off the heat.
  6. Allow the mixture to cool.
  7. Once cool, pour the nettle tea into jars and store them in the refrigerator.

Note: The nettle tea will be good for about a week when stored in the fridge.
