Easy Fish Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Frozen breaded fish filets
  • Buns
  • â…“ C Mayo
  • 3tbsp Spicy sauce (sriracha or rooster sauce) (to taste)
  • 1tbsp Relish
  • 1tsp Horseradish
  • Salsa to taste

Instructions:

  1. Put frozen fish filets in air fryer at 380 degrees for 12 minutes. After 12 minutes, flip and put back in for 5 more minutes.
  2. Butter insides of bun. Fry on a smoking hot skillet until toasted to taste. Do this when the fish is almost ready to take out.
  3. Mayo: Mix 1/3C mayo, 1tsp horseradish, 3tbsp rooster sauce, and 1tbsp relish together in a bowl.
  4. Put it all together and away you go! Top with salsa

Notes:

You can use any spicy sauce you like in place of sriracha or rooster sauce.
