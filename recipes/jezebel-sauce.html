Jezebel Sauce Recipe

About this Recipe

Sweet and spicy sauce used as an appetizer over cream cheese and a compliment to pork.

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Apple Jelly
  • 1 Cup Pineapple Jelly or Apricot
  • 2 Tbsp Dry Mustard or mustard
  • 1 Tbsp Prepared Horseradish or more if you like
  • 1 tsp . fresh cracked black pepper

Instructions

  1. Dump all ingredients in mixing bowl and mix well.
  2. Drizzle over a cream cheese ball.

Notes

You can adjust the sweetness and spiciness to your taste by using different ratios of apple and pineapple jelly, or adjusting the amount of horseradish and black pepper.
