Apple Cider Vinegar concentrate

Serves: N/A

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time: 1 hour

Total time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 cups honey
  • 1 tbsp whole cloves
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 1 tbsp peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp salt (canning or table)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine all of the ingredients and bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  3. After an hour, remove the pot from the heat and let it cool for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean glass jar.
  5. Allow the mixture to cool completely before covering the jar with a lid and storing it in the refrigerator.

Serving suggestion

Mix 3 tablespoons of the Apple Cider Vinegar concentrate in a glass of water and serve. The concentrate can also be used as a base for salad dressing, marinades, and other recipes.

Note: The Apple Cider Vinegar concentrate will keep in the refrigerator for up to 6 months.
